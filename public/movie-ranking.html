The Best animated movies according to Lalo

My top 3 animated movies OAT

Spiderman into the spiderverse

This was a great movie with a great character developement
One of the greates lessons of the movie is that it is okay to be
Afraid but the important thing is to face that fear and trust yourself.

Kung fu panda

Another movie with great character developement, Po has to
rebuild himself, change all the things he hates about himself
and work hard to became what he always dreamed of.

Batman Lego

Batman is a tough guy, that can do everything by himself,
but he has a lonely life, he doesn't like to open up with others
or allow himself to love someone or maintain a relationship
with anyone. At the end he learns that it's good to have someone
to support him and that he is not alone.
